Site Safety First

High winds, thunderstorms, and extreme temperatures threaten both personnel and equipment. Get alerts before conditions turn dangerous.

How It Works

Three Taps to
Weather Intelligence

Built for operators who need answers fast. No complex interfaces — just natural conversation on WhatsApp.

1

Message "hi" to the bot

The bot greets you by name, confirms your site, and presents the weather menu instantly.

2

Choose Current or Forecast

Reply 1 for live weather at your location, or 2 for a specific date up to 16 days ahead.

3

Share Location or Type City

Send your live location via WhatsApp's pin button, or type any city name. Get detailed wind, temperature, and precipitation data in seconds.

Live Weather Report

Temperature, humidity, cloud cover, pressure, and multi-level wind speeds (10m, 80m, 120m, 180m) — critical for turbine and panel assessments.

16-Day Hourly Forecast

Plan maintenance windows with precision. Hourly breakdowns show temperature, rain probability, wind speed, and conditions for any date you choose.

Broadcast to Teams

Operators can send urgent weather alerts to all site engineers or specific groups instantly by site operators.

Technical Capabilities

Everything Under
the Hood

Powered by Open-Meteo weather data and Twilio WhatsApp API. Built for reliability at remote renewable sites.

01

Multi-Level Wind Data

Wind speeds at 10m, 80m, 120m, and 180m heights — essential for wind farm turbine assessments and crane operations.

02

IST Timezone Native

All forecasts are delivered in Asia/Kolkata time. No mental math for shift planners working across Indian sites.

03

Geocoded City Search

Type any city name worldwide. The bot resolves it to precise coordinates and fetches local weather instantly.

04

Live Location Sharing

Drop a WhatsApp location pin from anywhere on site. Get hyperlocal weather for that exact GPS coordinate.

05

Group Broadcasts

Operators send targeted alerts to specific site groups or all users. Perfect for emergency weather warnings.

06

Full Chat History

The system stores every conversation. Review what weather data was shared, when, and with whom for compliance.
